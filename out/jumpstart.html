The First Week Guide

Seven days of exercises to get unstuck.

Free. No commitment.

What's inside

Seven exercises, one per day. Each takes 20–30 minutes. By the end of the week you'll have named what's actually stopping you — and have a clearer picture of what comes next.

  • 1Day 1: The fear inventory — name what's actually stopping you
  • 2Day 2: The identity audit — who are you outside the job?
  • 3Day 3: The spouse conversation — a structured framework to start it
  • 4Day 4: The freedom budget — what you'll actually spend, not what you think
  • 5Day 5: The social map — where your connections really come from
  • 6Day 6: The trial week — design one week of your retirement life
  • 7Day 7: The decision — what's the real blocker, and is it real?
