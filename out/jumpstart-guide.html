The First Week Guide

Get Clear. Get Started. Get Moving.

A companion to The Anti-Retirement Guide by Nick Constantinou

What Is This?

This is your 30-day action plan to go from "scared and uncertain" to "clear and ready." It complements the book "The Anti-Retirement Guide" but stands alone as a free resource.

Who Is This For?

  • You're 50-65 years old
  • You've done the math but you're still scared
  • Your spouse isn't ready (or you're not sure how to talk to them)
  • You want to design your next chapter—not just stop working
  • You're ready to do the work

When to Get Help

Consider professional help if:
  • You're overwhelmed by the numbers
  • Your spouse won't align
  • You're dealing with complex situations (health, divorce, estate)
  • You want a second opinion
Types of professionals:
  • Certified Financial Planner (CFP)
  • Fee-only financial advisor
  • Retirement coach
  • Therapist/counselor

Keep Going

This is just the beginning. The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. You've taken the first step by downloading this guide. Now take the next step. What's ONE thing you can do this week to move toward your next chapter? Write it down. Then do it.
