The First Week Guide

Seven days of exercises to get unstuck.

A companion to The Anti-Retirement Guide by Nick Constantinou

How this works: Seven exercises, one per day. Each takes 20–30 minutes. By the end of the week you'll have named what's actually stopping you — and have a clearer picture of what comes next. Do them in order. Don't rush them.

Day 3The Spouse Conversation

A structured framework to start the conversation.

Ask your partner: "What does your ideal day look like when we're retired?"

Don't interrupt. Don't judge. Just listen and write down what they say.

Ask: "What's the thing you're most worried about?"

Day 4The Freedom Budget

What you'll actually spend — not what you think you should.

Write your three numbers honestly:

Survival Budget (the minimum): £_______ /month

Comfort Budget (the good life): £_______ /month

Freedom Budget (everything you want): £_______ /month

Now ask: does your current plan support at least the survival number?
